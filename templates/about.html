Yes, you can learn at your own pace with lifetime access to all course materials.

Do I get a certificate?+

Absolutely. After completing a course, you’ll receive a verifiable, shareable certificate.

Is there any cost to join Ascend?+

Many of our foundational courses are free. Premium content and mentorship come with affordable pricing plans.

Can I access Ascend on mobile devices?+

Yes. Ascend is fully responsive and works seamlessly across desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.

How do I track my progress?+

Once enrolled, your dashboard shows real-time progress tracking, completion stats, and skill checkpoints.
